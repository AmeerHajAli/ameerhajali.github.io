Ameer Haj-Ali is an engineer and researcher in AI and AI infrastructure, having led Platform, Infrastructure, and Gen AI Engineering at Anyscale, Inc. During his tenure, he spearheaded critical projects like Anyscale's General Availability and new AI infrastructure. Ameer completed his Ph.D. in CS at UC Berkeley in just two years, working with Profs. Ion Stoica and Krste Asanovic. He was valedictorian for both his B.Sc. and M.Sc. at the Technion, and his research spans AI and Distributed Systems. He received multiple awards/fellowships for his scholastic achievements and publications in NeurIPS, CGO, OSDI, DATE, NetSoft, FCCM, TCAS-I, DAC, MLSys.
